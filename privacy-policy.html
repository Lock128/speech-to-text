Privacy Policy for HC VfL Speech to Text

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects and processes the following information:

  • Audio Recordings: We record audio when you use the speech-to-text feature
  • Transcribed Text: We convert your audio recordings to text
  • Technical Data: Basic app usage and error logs for functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Convert your speech recordings to text
  • Send transcribed messages via email
  • Improve app functionality and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Audio recordings are temporarily stored on secure AWS servers
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Audio files are automatically deleted after processing
  • We do not share your data with third parties

4. Permissions

Microphone Access: Required to record audio for speech-to-text conversion. Audio is only recorded when you actively use the recording feature.

5. Data Retention

Audio recordings are deleted immediately after transcription. Transcribed text is only retained as long as necessary for email delivery.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Revoke microphone permissions in your device settings
  • Contact us to request data deletion

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: lockhead@lockhead.info

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
